# Papers that some algorithms are based on

## Surface Simplification Using Quadric Error Metrics. 
* Authors: Michael Garland and Paul S. Heckbert.
* Source: https://mgarland.org/files/papers/quadrics.pdf
* Idea: 
  * QEM simplification algorithm
  * Extend the space to include uv coordinate (x, y, z, u, v) in R5

## Multi-resolution 3D Approximations for rendering complex scenes
* Authors: Jarek R. Rossignac and Paul Borrel.
* Source: 
  * Modeling in Computer Graphics: Methods and Applications. Pages 455-465.
  * https://faculty.cc.gatech.edu/~jarek/papers/VertexClustering.pdf
* Idea:
  * Vertex clustering simplification algorithm
